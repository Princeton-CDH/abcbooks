Archduke Charles
Sold by: A. Keith

THE
CHILD'S ALPHABET,
EMBLEMATICALLY
Described and Embellished
BY
TWENTY-FOUR PICTURES.
BROUGHT INTO
EASY VERSE,
FOR THE
TENDER CAPACITIES
OF
Y0UNG READERS.
The whole contrived
TO ALLURE CHILDREN
INTO THE
LOVE OF LEARNING.

GLASGOW,
PRINTED JBY J. AND M. ROBERTSON,
1805.

A
The Tree of Life,
And Adam's Wife.
In Adam's Fall,
We sinned all.

B
Pray read the Bible,
Be not idle.
This Book attend,
Thy Life amend.

C
The Cat and Fiddle,
High diddle, diddle,
The Cat doth play,
And after slay.

D
The careful Beast,
Pleaseth best.
The dog doth bite,
A Thief at Night.

E
The Eagle’s Eye,
Mounts the Sky.
An Eagl’e Flight,
Is out of Sight.

F
A Fool will brag,
Tho’ not worth a rag.
The idle Fool,
Is whipt at School.

G
Thus flies Time,
Make much of thine.
As runs the Glass,
Man's Life doth pass.

H
Strive Heart and Hand
To obey Command.
My Book and Heart,
Shall never part.

J
Job feels the Rod,
Yet blesses God.
His present Pain,
Was future Gain.

K
The King obey,
So doth God say.
King Charles the good,
Was of high Blood.

L
Sweet Innocence
Gives no offence.
The Lion bold
The Lamb doth hold.

M
How kind is Heaven
Thus to have given.
The Moon at Night
To Shine so bright.

N
The lofty Tree
Is here you see.
The Nightingale doth sing,
Most lovely in the Spring.

O
The Royal Oak
Is not a Joke.
The Oak we sing,
Did save the King.

P
Peter's Sins
Repentance brings.
Peter denies
His Lord, and cries.

Q
Quaint words and behaviour,
Gains the King's Favour.
Queen Esther came in state,
The Jews to save from fate.

R
From Earth we come,
To Earth return.
Rachel doth mourn
For her first-born.

S
Anointed David
Well behaved.
Samuel anoints,
Whom God appoints.

T
The Scythe and Glass
Shew Time will pass.
Time cuts down all,
Both great and small.

U
Wickedness brings on,
A doleful Song.
Uriah's beauteous Wife,
Makes David seek his Life.

W
In the Whale's Belly Jonah lay,
Three whole Nights and Days.
Whales in the Sea
God's Voice obey.

X
A King from Death
Can't save his Breath.
Xerxes the Great did die.
And so must you and I.

Y
Death and his Dart,
Strikes the youngest Heart.
Youth forward skips,
Death soonest nips.

Z
A willing mind for good,
Makes Intention understood.
Zaccheus climes the Tree,
His Lord to see.

ADVERTISEMENT.
To all the pretty little Masters Blisses,
And all the pretty little Misses,
Who are in London Town,
And Country up and down,
To many hundreds more,
From three Year, to threescore.
Come buy this little Book,
And on it deign to look,
Then soon will come another,
For size will be his Brother;
Pretty Prints it will contain,
You’ll look it o’er and o’er again.
FINIS.

GLASGOW,
Printed by J. and M. Robertson, Saltmarket, 1805.

Emperor of Russia
