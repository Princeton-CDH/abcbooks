<head>A</head>
  <lg>
  <l>A is the Artist,</l>
  <l>Who drew in these pages-</l>
  <l>He's not had his hair cut-</l>
  <l>For ages and ages.</l>
  </lg>
<head>B</head>
  <lg>
  <l>B is the baby</l>
  <l>Who is crowing with joy,</l>
  <l>But isn't that diggie</l>
  <l>A bad, greedy boy?</l>
  </lg>
<head>C</head>
  <lg>
  <l>C is for Cook</l>
  <l>Bow how very absurd,</l>
  <l>For such a large cook to cook</l>
  <l>Such a small bird</l>
  </lg>
<head>D</head>
  <lg>
  <l>D is for Doctor</l>
  <l>Who comes when you're ill</l>
  <l>He looks at your tongue,</l>
  <l>Then gives you a pill.</l>
  </lg>
<head>E</head>
  <lg>
  <l>E is for Errand Boy,</l>
  <l>Who loves to watch dogs fight,</l>
  <l>his master’s meat is eaten</l>
  <l>by the bow-wows on the right.</l>
  </lg>
<head>F</head>
  <lg>
  <l>F is for Fireman,</l>
  <l>This one is very brave,</l>
  <l>He's climbed up the ladder</l>
  <l>Poor pussy to save.</l>
  </lg>
<head>G</head>
  <lg>
  <l>G  is for Gardener,</l>
  <l>His enemies are snails:</l>
  <l>He catches them with salt you know,</l>
  <l>Then drowns hem in pails.</l>
  </lg>
<head>H</head>
  <lg>
  <l>H is for Horse,</l>
  <l>No wonder Cabby smiles ;</l>
  <l>It’s only taken three short hours</l>
  <l>To travel three long miles.</l>
  </lg>
<head>I</head>
  <lg>
  <l>I is for Italian,</l>
  <l>Who is fond of Macaroni</l>
  <l>We don't know what his name is</l>
  <l>We rather think it's Tony.</l>
  </lg>
<head>J</head>
  <lg>
  <l>J is for Judy</l>
  <l>Her husband's name is Punch,</l>
  <l>And  together with</l>
  <l>dog Toby</l>
  <l>They are a happy bunch.</l>
  </lg>
<head>K</head>
  <lg>
  <l>K is for Kitchen Maid,</l>
  <l>Who's dropped the buttered scones.</l>
  <l>We hope she hasn't hurt herself</l>
  <l>Or broken any bones.</l>
  </lg>
<head>L</head>
  <lg>
  <l>L is for Laundress</l>
  <l>Her washing's very fine</l>
  <l>When Fido fell into the tub</l>
  <l>She hung him on the line.</l>
  </lg>
<head>M</head>
  <lg>
  <l>M is for Milkman,</l>
  <l>Why is he smiling so?</l>
  <l>He likes a rainy day, for then</l>
  <l>The milk is sure to grow.</l>
  </lg>
<head>N</head>
  <lg>
  <l>N is for Nurse</l>
  <l>Who says (and she knows)</l>
  <l>That Wright's Coal Tar Soap</l>
  <l>is as pure as a rose.</l>
  </lg>
<head>O</head>
  <lg>
  <l>O is for Office-Boy,</l>
  <l>in blue serge suit so neat,</l>
  <l>Who points to the point of this picture,</l>
  <l>Do you see it on that seat ?</l>
  </lg>
<head>Q</head>
  <lg>
  <l>Q is for Quarrel—</l>
  <l>To be friends is better far—</l>
  <l>We don’t like naughty children,</l>
  <l>So let’s turn the page to R.</l>
  </lg>
<head>R</head>
  <lg>

  <l>The doggie runs beside and barks</l>
  <l>To make the gee-gee go</l>
  </lg>

  <l>Who brings pockets full of Toys,</l>
