Put the wig in the box.
Will you go to bed? Give me my new hat. Mend this pen for me. I like a cup and ball.
Will you have a tart? Look at the red Cow. Do not hurt the Fly. O! what a line Nag!
Is that a new gun ?
Let me fly my kite.
Poor Bob fell in a pit.
See how the Ducks swim.
Give me a knife and fork. Let me comb out my hair. Will you brush my coat. Let us go out to play.
Do not ink your shirt.
I wish I had a nice pear. Here is a fine plum cake. Now be a good child.

  <l>Quite to my taste.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag3" ana="C">
<lg>

  <l>But don’t ask for a slice,</l>
  <l>For it serves to maintain</l>
  <l>A whole nation of Mice.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag4" ana="D">
<lg>
  <l>D , was Dick Dump,</l>
  <l>Who did nothing but eat.</l>
  <l>And would leave book and play</l>
  <l>For a nice bit of meat.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag5" ana="E">
<lg>
  <l>E, is an Egg</l>
  <l>In a basket with more,</l>
  <l>Which Jenny will sell</l>
  <l>For a shilling a score.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag6" ana="F">
<lg>
  <l>F was a Forester,</l>
  <l>Dress’d all in green,</l>
  <l>With a Cap of fine fur,</l>
  <l>Like a King or a Queen.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag7" ana="G">
<lg>
  <l>And G, was a Greyhound,</l>
  <l>As swift as the wind ;</l>
  <l>In the race or the course</l>
  <l>Left all others behind.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag8" ana="H">
<lg>
  <l>H, was a Hoyden,</l>
  <l>Not like you, nor like me,</l>
  <l>For she tumbled about</l>
  <l>Like the waves of the sea.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag9" ana="I">
<lg>
  <l>I, was the Ice</l>
  <l>On which William would skate,</l>
  <l>So up went his heels.</l>
  <l>And down went his pate.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag10" ana="J">
<lg>
  <l>J, was Joe Jenkins,</l>
  <l>Who play’d on the fiddle,</l>
  <l>And began twenty tunes,</l>
  <l>But left off in the middle.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag11" ana="K">
<lg>
  <l>K , was a Kitten,</l>
  <l>Who jump’d at a Cork,</l>
  <l>And learn'd to eat mice</l>
  <l>Without plate, knife, or fork.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag12" ana="L">
<lg>
  <l>And L, was the Lady,</l>
  <l>Who made him so wise;</l>
  <l>But he tore her long train,</l>
  <l>And she cried out her eyes.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag13" ana="M">
<lg>
  <l>M, was Miss Mira,</l>
  <l>Who turn'd in her toes,</l>
  <l>And poked down her head</l>
  <l>Till her knees met her nose.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag14" ana="N">
<lg>
  <l>And N, Mr. Nobody,</l>
  <l>Just come from France,</l>
  <l>Said he'd set her upright,</l>
  <l>And teach her to dance.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag15" ana="O">
<lg>

</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag16" ana="P">
<lg>
  <l>P, was a Pilgrim,</l>
  <l>With a staff in his hand,</l>
  <l>Return’d weary and faint</l>
  <l>From a far distant land.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag17" ana="Q">
<lg>

  <l>I've heard say,</l>

  <l>Just dress’d for the play.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag18" ana="R">

  <l>Calling so loud,</l>

  <l>To look at the crowd.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag19" ana="S">

</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag20" ana="T">

  <l>Hast’ning on while ’twas light,</l>

  <l>To rest in at night.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag21" ana="U">

  <l>Saved Bell, t’ other day</l>

  <l>Whilst she turn’d the new hay.</l>
</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag22" ana="V">

</lg>
</div>

<div type="alpha-group" xml:id="ag23" ana="W">

</lg>
</div>

</div>
