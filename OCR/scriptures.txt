


As the first impressions that are formed on the infant mind are fonnd to he most permanent. It is the design of this little work by means of Graphical illustrations accompanying each Letter of the Alphabet to impress on the earliest dawning of reason clear & distinct ideas of the principle characteristics of the sacred Scriptures.
